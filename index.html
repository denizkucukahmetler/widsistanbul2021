WiDS ISTANBUL

WiDS Istanbul is an independent event organized by Aslihan Demirkaya, Öznur Taştan and Özge Özmen to coincide with the annual Women in Data Science (WiDS) Worldwide conference hosted by Stanford University and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS events, which feature outstanding women doing outstanding work.
